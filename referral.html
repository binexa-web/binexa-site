Referral & Rewards

Binexa (BNX) features a transparent referral and rewards system designed to encourage ecosystem growth while maintaining long-term sustainability and fairness.

Referral Program

The Binexa referral system rewards users for expanding the ecosystem through genuine participation and network growth.

  • Earn rewards by referring new users
  • Referral rewards distributed automatically
  • No fake volume or artificial incentives
  • Fully tracked on-chain via smart contracts

Reward Distribution

Rewards are distributed in USDT based on predefined rules to ensure fairness and prevent abuse.

  • Rewards tied to real ecosystem activity
  • No manual reward manipulation
  • Controlled emission to avoid inflation
  • Transparent reward calculation logic

Long-Term Incentives

Binexa prioritizes long-term engagement by aligning referral rewards with staking, vesting, and ecosystem performance.

  • Rewards encourage long-term holding
  • Referral incentives linked with vesting
  • Discourages short-term dumping behavior
  • Supports sustainable ecosystem growth
