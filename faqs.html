Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about Binexa (BNX), its ecosystem, token mechanics, and long-term vision.

What is Binexa (BNX)?

Binexa (BNX) is a utility and governance token designed to power the Binexa DeFi ecosystem, including staking, vesting, gaming rewards, and referrals.

Which blockchain does BNX run on?

BNX is deployed on the BNB Smart Chain (BEP-20), ensuring fast transactions and low fees.

Is BNX supply fixed?

Yes. BNX has a fixed maximum supply with controlled circulation to prevent inflation and ensure long-term sustainability.

How does staking work?

Users can stake BNX to earn rewards through automated smart contracts. Rewards may be compounded to maximize long-term returns.

What is vesting and cliff?

Vesting releases tokens gradually over time after a cliff period, preventing early dumping and protecting ecosystem stability.

How does the referral system work?

Users earn rewards by referring new participants. All rewards are distributed transparently via smart contracts.

Are rewards manually controlled?

No. All rewards, staking, vesting, and referral distributions are fully automated and on-chain.

Is Binexa focused on short-term profits?

No. Binexa is designed for long-term ecosystem growth, discouraging speculation and encouraging sustainable participation.

Can smart contracts be modified?

Core token and reward contracts are immutable or governed transparently to ensure trust and security.

Where can I learn more about Binexa?

You can explore the ecosystem pages, token overview, and official community channels for detailed information and updates.
