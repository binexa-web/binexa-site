Vesting & Cliff

Binexa (BNX) follows a transparent vesting and cliff-based token release model designed to prevent early dumping, protect long-term holders, and ensure sustainable ecosystem growth.

Token Lock & Cliff Period

All BNX tokens acquired through the Binexa ecosystem are automatically locked via smart contracts to ensure controlled circulation and long-term stability.

  • Tokens are locked immediately after purchase
  • No admin or manual control over locked tokens
  • Cliff period prevents early token dumping
  • Fully enforced on-chain via smart contracts

Vesting Mechanism

Vesting ensures gradual token release over time, aligning incentives between users and the Binexa ecosystem.

  • Vesting starts after the cliff period ends
  • Up to 2% of locked BNX unlocks monthly
  • Fully automated and transparent process
  • No manual claiming or manipulation possible
